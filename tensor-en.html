Tensor Framework for Measuring and Optimizing Human Dignity Realization

A Mathematical Foundation for the Happiness Index in N-Dimensional Value Space

Author: Boris Mayorov, Independent Researcher

Abstract: This paper introduces a tensor-based mathematical framework for measuring and optimizing the realization of human dignity in an N-dimensional value space. Human happiness is defined as the norm of realized values obtained through the action of a realization tensor on the potential value vector. The framework is invariant under dimensional expansion and applies at individual, regional, and planetary scales.

1. Introduction

Modern civilization lacks a formal measurable operator describing the realization of human dignity. We establish dignity as an invariant intrinsic property of every human:

$$D = 1$$

This formalism describes how systems transform human potential into realized values.

2. Value Space

Define an N-dimensional value space \(V = \mathbb{R}^N\). Each human has a potential value vector \(P^i \in V\), where \(i = 1, \dots, N\). Components represent Knowledge, Strength, Economic Resources, etc.

3. Realization Tensor

Define the realization tensor \(R^i_j\), where \(0 \le R^i_j \le 1\). Realized values \(V^i\) are calculated as:

$$V^i = R^i_j P^j$$

This tensor incorporates internal (biological), regional (political), and planetary factors.

4. Metric Tensor and Happiness

Define the metric tensor \(g_{ij}\) which defines the weighting of value space. The Happiness Index \(H\) is defined as the scalar norm of realized values:

$$H = \sqrt{g_{ij} V^i V^j} = \sqrt{g_{ij} R^i_k R^j_l P^k P^l}$$

5. Hierarchical Structure

The realization tensor can be decomposed across scales:

$$R^i_j = R^i_k (\text{individual}) \cdot R^k_l (\text{region}) \cdot R^l_j (\text{planet})$$

6. AI Alignment Principle

An aligned artificial intelligence system must prioritize the optimization of the realization tensor rather than simple economic output:

$$\max \left( \sqrt{g_{ij} R^i_k R^j_l P^k P^l} \right)$$

7. Conclusion

The framework enables objective measurement of human dignity realization at all scales, providing a formal optimization target for an AI-aligned civilization.

Keywords: Human dignity, happiness index, tensor formalism, AI alignment, value space.
